Tool Definitions v
Define the tools your app exposes as JSON schemas. Each tool maps to a function the AI can call on behalf of the student.
UI Embedding >
Your UI URL is loaded in a sandboxed iframe. Use postMessage for communication with ChatBridge.
Authentication >
OAuth2 is recommended. ChatBridge handles the token exchange and passes credentials securely.
Compliance Requirements >
All apps must attest to COPPA and FERPA compliance. SOC 2 and VPAT are strongly recommended.
Review Process >
Submissions are reviewed by district admins within 5 business days. You'll receive email notifications on status changes.
