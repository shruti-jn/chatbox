20. Motion & Interaction

Motion philosophy: "Balanced and purposeful — motion guides focus and confirms actions, with playful touches for app materialization moments." Every animation serves a functional purpose. K-2 mode adds 20% to all durations. Respects prefers-reduced-motion.

Accessibility: Reduced Motion

When prefers-reduced-motion: reduce is active, all transform-based animations are replaced with opacity transitions. Auto-playing animations (shimmer) are paused. Functional indicators (loading state, safety alerts) remain active but simplified. Celebrations (confetti) are disabled entirely. All motion is teacher-configurable per classroom.
